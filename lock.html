The Crisis

Bangladesh's hospitals
are running on empty.

At DMCH and similar public institutions, the patient-to-doctor ratio is catastrophic. Every consultation is a race against time — and patients pay the price.

Overburdened Physicians

Doctors are forced into 3–5 minute consultations, unable to build patient context. Every second wasted re-explaining history is a second not spent on diagnosis or care.

Lost Paper Records

Patients routinely lose prescriptions and test results. Without digital continuity, each visit starts from scratch, leading to repeated tests and dangerous medication errors.

Zero Clinical Continuity

No persistent patient memory means no long-term care coordination. Chronic conditions go unmanaged. Follow-ups are forgotten. The system fails the most vulnerable.

The Solution

11 AI agents.
One seamless system.

OmniCare inserts intelligent automation at every touchpoint of the patient journey — without disrupting existing hospital workflows.

Voice-First Clinical Intake

Veena conducts a full Bangla-language symptom interview in natural conversation. Real-time STT transcription generates a structured clinical summary before the doctor calls the patient's name — saving 4–6 minutes per consultation.

Doctor's Live Dashboard

Pre-filtered critical symptom alerts delivered the moment intake finishes. Zero context lost. Consultations start at diagnosis.

Persistent Memory Graph

Every visit, prescription, and diagnosis stored in a secure knowledge graph. Total clinical continuity across every hospital visit.

Vision Rx — OCR

AI reads handwritten prescriptions and digitizes medication schedules instantly. No prescription is ever lost again.

Offline-First Hospital Server

We deploy a high-efficiency, mid-range server directly inside each hospital. This means 100% uptime, capacity for over 1 billion patient records stored locally, and zero dependency on internet connectivity. OmniCare works even when the power grid struggles.

Meet the Guide

Veena

The face patients trust. The intelligence that transforms intake.

Veena
3D Clinical Guide · OmniCare
Bangla NLU Voice-First Empathic AI Always-On

She speaks when doctors can't.

Veena is OmniCare's voice-first clinical companion — a 3D avatar designed to feel warm, approachable, and trustworthy to patients of every literacy level and socioeconomic background. She doesn't just collect symptoms; she builds genuine rapport from the very first sentence.

When a patient scans the QR code at reception, Veena greets them and initiates a natural Bangla conversation. She listens to their concerns, asks intelligent follow-up questions, and captures everything — symptoms, timeline, medication history, allergies — with the precision of a trained medical scribe.

Before the patient ever enters the doctor's room, Veena has already delivered a complete structured clinical brief to the physician's dashboard. The doctor walks in informed. The consultation starts at diagnosis, not at "what brings you in today?"

In field testing with 10–15 real patients at a Dhaka hospital, the response was unanimous: patients felt heard. Many said it was the first time anyone at a hospital had listened to their full story.
