OmniCare

Addressing the healthcare crisis in Bangladesh with a companion that follows the patient, not the file.

The Problem

Public hospitals like DMCH face a severe throughput crisis. Doctors are overburdened, consults are rushed to 3-5 minutes, and patients often lose paper records or forget verbal instructions. Every visit starts from zero because there is no persistent clinical context, leading to medication errors and poor adherence.

The OmniCare Solution

OmniCare is an 11-agent AI system that bridges this gap. It provides a 3D avatar guide (Veena) for Bangla-voice intake, an automated clinical brief for doctors to save time, and a persistent memory graph that tracks a patient's entire history across visits.

How it works for the patient:

Upon arrival, patients scan a QR Code at reception, which launches our PWA immediately. There, they meet Veena, who initiates a natural Bangla conversation. Veena listens to their symptoms and problems, transcribing them in real-time into a structured clinical summary. This summary is then instantly delivered to the doctor's dashboard, pre-filtered for critical symptoms, so the consultation starts with 100% context.

  • Voice-First Intake: Veena handles symptoms and history in Bangla, ensuring clarity for all literacy levels.
  • Vision Rx: AI extraction of handwritten prescriptions to digitize medication schedules.
  • Dedicated Hospital Servers: We install a high-efficiency, mid-range server directly inside each hospital. This ensures 100% uptime and the capacity to manage over a billion patient records locally, with zero reliance on the internet.
